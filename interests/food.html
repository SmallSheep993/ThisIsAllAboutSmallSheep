Travel & Gastronomy

Exploring the world one plate at a time—where culture meets culinary tradition.

A Global Palette

Travel is my favorite way to expand my horizons. Having served as a Multicultural Club Vice President, I believe that food is the ultimate universal language. Whether it is a street food stall in Asia or a traditional bistro in Europe, every meal tells a story about the history, geography, and soul of a place.

Travel Philosophy

Local Authenticity

Tradition • Ingredients • History

I seek out family-owned establishments and "hidden gems" that keep traditional recipes alive, valuing authenticity over tourist trends.

Culture & Community

Connection • Exchange • Diversity

Eating together is a form of community. I love how culinary traditions bring people from different backgrounds to the same table.

The Explorer's Mindset

Curiosity • Adventure • Flavor

Much like exploring a new codebase, I approach new cuisines with curiosity, eager to understand the "architecture" of different flavors.
