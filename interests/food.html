The Joy of Eating

If you asked me what takes up most of the space in my phone’s gallery, I wouldn’t even need to think about it — it’s food. My camera roll is basically a collection of meals I’ve loved.

I just really love food. It’s one of those simple joys in life that never gets old. I enjoy trying different cuisines, playing around with cooking styles, and discovering new flavors. My dream is to taste the cuisine of every country in the world.

But no matter how much I explore, Chinese food will always feel like home to me. It’s the taste I grew up with, the flavors that feel familiar and comforting. Nothing quite beats that.
