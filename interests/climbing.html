Rock climbing

Bouldering, indoor climbing, and a bit of outdoor.

My climbing story

I'm not an elite climber — but climbing is part of my life. Most of my time is in the gym; I've only been outdoors once, and it gave me huge respect for the sport.

I'm working through V5 problems: strength alone isn't enough — you have to read the wall and position your body. It's a puzzle that needs persistence and a calm mind.

Goal: keep showing up, keep learning, and get truly good at it.
