My Climbing Story

I would definitely not call myself an elite climber by any means lol, but climbing has become a part of my life. Most of my time is spent in the indoor gym, where the controlled environment allows me to focus purely on technique and strength. While I have only ventured into outdoor climbing once, that single experience gave me a profound respect for the raw nature of the sport.

Currently, I am working through V5 problems. For me, V5 represents that sweet spot where brute strength isn't enough—you need to start "reading" the wall and optimizing your body positioning. It’s a physical puzzle that demands both persistence and a calm mind.

Looking ahead, I hope to continue this journey, deepening my passion for the sport. My goal is simple: to keep showing up, keep learning, and eventually become truly good at it.
